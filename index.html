LLMs in Business - From Prompt Engineering to AI Agents

Offered at Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen

Course Outline

Course Code MIS3012
Course Title LLMs in Business - From Prompt Engineering to AI Agents
Units 3
Description This course engages in the world of AI with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs) and their role in business. It includes lab-heavy and hands-on sessions designed to bridge the gap between management and technical worlds.

Learning Outcomes

  • Comprehend AI and LLMs.
  • Optimize and engineer prompts.
  • Instruct LLMs for valuable content creation.
  • Develop multi-modal content using multi-agent modes.
